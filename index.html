Ground rules

Safe vs. Sending. Read-only tools: run freely. Anything that SENDS to fans drafts only and waits for human approval. No auto-send to fans, ever.

Caption boundary — hard line. Claude studies top captions and WHY they worked (hook, structure, urgency, timing) and suggests non-explicit angles. Claude will NOT write erotic or sexually explicit wording. Breaking this can get the account banned.

One per day. The page enforces it: one check-off per manager per day, each build only once, and check-offs can't be edited or deleted after submitting.
