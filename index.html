How it works

Inside the Claude desktop app (Cowork) · ~10–20 min
1

Open Cowork

Open the Cowork app on your computer — the Claude app you’re in right now. Everything happens here. You don’t open anything else, and you don’t need to know any tech.

2

Get your skill

On today’s card, tap Get the skill. A little file downloads. In Cowork, add it once — now Cowork knows how to do that whole job for you.

3

Say your model’s name

Tell Cowork: “run it for [your model].” That’s the whole command — type it or say it out loud. It pulls the numbers and writes the report itself.

4

Check it off here

Copy what Cowork made, come back to this page, paste it in the box, add a screenshot, and press the green button. You’re done for the day.
